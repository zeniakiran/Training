💬 Words That Inspire

Great people throughout history have left behind powerful words. Here are some of our favourites.

Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein once said: Imagination is more important than knowledge.

The measure of intelligence is the ability to change.

Malala Yousafzai

Malala reminded the world that one child, one teacher, one book, one pen can change the world.

I raise up my voice — not so that I can shout, but so that those without a voice can be heard.

Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela believed that education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.

It always seems impossible until it is done.

Marie Curie

Marie Curie once said: Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood.

I was taught that the way of progress was neither swift nor easy.
